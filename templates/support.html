Frequently Asked Questions
Navigate to Loan Discovery from your dashboard, browse available products, and click Apply Now. You'll need an approved KYC before your application is processed.
KYC verification typically takes 24–48 hours after all required documents are submitted. You'll receive a notification once your status is updated.
Go to EMI Schedule from your dashboard. Click "Pay from Wallet" on any pending EMI. Ensure your wallet has sufficient balance before proceeding.
Pay your EMIs on time, keep credit utilization below 30%, and avoid multiple loan applications in a short period. Check your Credit Rewards page for personalized tips.
Go to Wallet, click Withdraw, and enter your bank details (bank name, account number, IFSC code) along with the amount. Withdrawals are processed within 1–2 business days.
